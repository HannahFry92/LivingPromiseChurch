2019 Sermons

Current Sermon Series: Giving up sin and what Jesus gave up to take our sin away.

The tradition of giving up something during Lent goes back to the first centuries of the church as Christians fasted for 40 days before Easter. Today when you hear of people giving something up for Lent it is usually something fairly trivial: desserts, red meat, or social media. This year let us give up more than the trivial, let us seek to give up our sin. As we spend 40 days preparing our hearts for Jesus’ passion, let us focus on giving up our sin and what Jesus gave up to pay for our sin.
