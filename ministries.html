Ministries

Children

"Jesus said, "let the little children come to Me, and do not hinder them! For the kingdom of heaven belongs to such as these." Matthew 19:14

We have several events to especially strengthen the faith of our youngest members ages 2-10.

SAM3:10 Youth

"Speak, Lord, your servant is listening." 1 Samuel 3:10

Our youth group is named after 1 Samuel 3:10 where Samuel, just a teen himself, says to God, "Speak, Lord, your servant is listening." Our name serves as a reminder of why we exist: to gather together as servants to hear our Lord's voice. We do this through Bible Study, Service Projects, Trips and Outings. Please join us for our next event!

Family

"We love because He first loved us." 1 JOHN 4:19

Living Promise worships as a family inclusive church. Instead of separating kids and parents we involve all ages in the same worship. With something for everyone we worship together, not apart. We think this is a great way to strengthen people and families and we think you will too.

Ladies League

"Charm is deceptive, and beauty does not last; but a woman who fears the Lord will be greatly praised." Proverbs 31:30

This is a women's group that ministers to the needs of its members and its community going through life's changes, both good and bad.

Men's

"As iron sharpens iron, so a man sharpens the countenance of his friend." Proverbs 27:17

This is a men's group that ministers to the needs of its members and its community going through life's changes, both good and bad.
