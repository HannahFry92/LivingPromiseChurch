Marriage Retreat

Oct. 6, 2018

"Living the Promise of Marriage" is a refresher and retreat for your marriage. You and your spouse will have an opportunity to learn and work on some real skills to strengthen and enhance your marriage. Then you will have an opportunity to be on a date night to help you put those skills into practice and grow closer as a couple. Our focus for this Fall retreat is "Finding Meaning in Marriage." Our date night will be a candlelit dinner compete with white linens, chocolates, a fine meal provided by Little City Catering, and a selection of wines.
